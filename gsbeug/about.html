Historical Highlights

The Ghana Society of Biomedical Engineers – University of Ghana Local (GSBE-UG) was established in 2022 to serve as the primary representation for biomedical engineering students at the University of Ghana. Created as a local chapter of the Ghana Society of Biomedical Engineers (GSBE), GSBE-UG was founded to bridge the gap between students and the broader GSBE network. Its creation aimed to enhance student engagement with professional activities, facilitate skill development, and foster a strong community within the University’s Department of Biomedical Engineering.

Our Vision

GSBE-UG aspires to be a premier student organization in biomedical engineering, distinguished by our commitment to excellence and innovation. Our vision is to:

  • Empower Member: Equip our members with advanced skills and knowledge to excel in the biomedical engineering field.

  • Foster Connections: Facilitate meaningful professional relationships and networking opportunities.

  • Advance the Profession: Contribute to the growth and development of biomedical engineering in Ghana.

  • Drive Change: Cultivate a vibrant community that nurtures future leaders, driving positive change and elevating the role of biomedical engineering in healthcare and technology.

Our Mission

The mission of the Ghana Society of Biomedical Engineers – University of Ghana Local (GSBE-UG) is to serve as a vital representative and supporter of biomedical engineering students at the University of Ghana. Our core objectives are to:

  • Facilitate Communication: Act as a bridge between students and the Ghana Society of Biomedical Engineers (GSBE), ensuring effective communication and collaboration. .

  • Enhance Engagement: Actively involve students in GSBE activities, enriching their academic and professional development.

  • Promote Collaboration: Foster a collaborative and respectful environment among students, faculty, and professionals in the field of biomedical engineering.

  • Advance Skills: Provide members with opportunities for skill development through exchange programs and connections with global biomedical engineering students.

  • Secure Opportunities: Assist in securing internships and professional opportunities in partnership with GSBE and industry stakeholders.

  • Organize Events: Host educational events, including field trips, symposia, and forums, to expand members' knowledge and appreciation of the field.

  • Build Alumni Network: Develop a robust GSBE-UG Alumni network to support and advance the field of biomedical engineering within the University and throughout Ghana.
